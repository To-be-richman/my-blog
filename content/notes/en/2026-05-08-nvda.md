Everyone talks about GPUs.

Almost nobody talks about advanced packaging.

The AI infrastructure boom is no longer limited by compute demand alone.

CoWoS capacity, power availability, cooling systems, and transformer supply are quietly becoming critical constraints.

This is what most software-focused investors still fail to understand.

AI is becoming a heavy industry.

And heavy industries always hit physical bottlenecks eventually.
