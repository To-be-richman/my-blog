# AI & Semiconductor Strategy

Artificial intelligence and semiconductor strategy are reshaping the future of global technology.

The AI revolution is driving unprecedented demand for advanced chips, high-performance computing, and data infrastructure.

## Why Chips Matter

Semiconductors are now the foundation of:

- AI
- Cloud Computing
- Robotics
- Autonomous Driving

Countries and companies that dominate chip manufacturing will likely control the next era of technological power.

## Global Competition

The competition between:

- United States
- China
- Taiwan

is becoming increasingly important in semiconductor supply chains.

## Final Thoughts

AI is not only a software revolution.

It is also a semiconductor revolution.
